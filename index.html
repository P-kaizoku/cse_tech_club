About the Club

Who we are

Our Mission

We exist to bridge the gap between classroom academia and the real-world demands of the software industry. Every initiative — from beginner workshops to competitive hackathons — is designed to push students one step further.

Whether you're writing your first line of code or deploying production systems, there's a place for you here.

What We Do

  • 01 Run weekly coding sessions, workshops, and study circles
  • 02 Host inter-college hackathons and competitive programming contests
  • 03 Mentor students in open-source contributions and placement prep
  • 04 Collaborate with industry professionals through tech talks and panels
